# Privacy Policy

## Overview

Daily Spark is committed to your privacy. This app is designed with privacy-first principles.

---

## No Account Required

Daily Spark does NOT require:
- Email or username login
- Account creation
- Account activation
- Any kind of registration

You can use the app completely anonymously.

---

## No Cloud Storage

Your data is stored **only on your device**:
- No server uploads
- No cloud synchronization
- No backend storage
- No third-party data centers

---

## No Analytics or Tracking

Daily Spark does NOT:
- Track your habits or streaks
- Collect usage statistics
- Monitor what you track
- Send telemetry data
- Use Google Analytics or similar tools

---

## No Ads

Daily Spark has:
- No advertisements
- No sponsored content
- No banner ads
- No pop-up ads
- No tracking pixels

---

## No Data Collection

We do NOT collect:
- Your habit data
- Your name or email
- Your location
- Your device information
- Crash reports with personal data
- Any personal information

---

## Local-Only Storage

All data is stored locally on your Android device using:
- Device's secure local storage
- No encryption required (device-level security applies)
- Standard Android storage permissions

---

## Backup & Restore

You control your backups:
- Export backups as JSON files (you manage these files)
- Store them where you want (cloud, email, USB, etc.)
- Delete backups anytime
- We don't access your backups

---

## Notifications

Push notifications:
- Are completely optional
- Can be disabled anytime
- Are sent locally by your device
- Do NOT require internet connection
- Do NOT require app registration

---

## Data Safety & Responsibility

Important facts:
- You own your data
- Backup is YOUR responsibility
- We recommend regular JSON backups
- App uninstall deletes local data (unless you backup first)
- We are NOT liable for data loss
- Device-level security protects your data

---

## No Third-Party Services

Daily Spark does NOT use:
- Firebase analytics
- Crashlytics with data
- Third-party analytics
- Advertising networks
- Social media tracking

---

## Future Changes

If we ever change our privacy practices:
- We will notify users clearly
- No retroactive changes to past data
- You can stop using the app anytime

---

## Contact

If you have privacy concerns or questions:
- Open an issue on GitHub
- Do NOT expect a guaranteed response time

---

**Last Updated:** February 2026

Your data is yours. We don't want it, collect it, or share it.
