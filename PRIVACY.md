# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-01

Daily Spark is designed to be offline-first.

## Summary
- No data collection
- No analytics
- No ads
- No tracking
- Data stays on your device
- No third-party sharing

## Data Storage
Daily Spark stores your streaks and settings locally on your device.

## Third-Party Services
Daily Spark does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Sharing
Any sharing actions you initiate (for example, exporting or sharing content) are performed by you and handled by your device and chosen apps.

## Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated in the repository over time. The “Last updated” date will reflect the latest version.

## Contact
For questions or concerns, please open an issue on the project repository.
